I’m not going to sugarcoat myself, but it’s fair to say I’ve ended up on a better side of the internet. Reddit can be bad, but there’s a charm to it. You don’t worry about your follower count, do don’t think about monetization, you just post what you feel like. If it’s high quality, people see it no matter you’re famous or not. They exaggerate little things, straight up make up fake news, spreading wrong beliefs, and anything to increase their engagement.

I’m writing about a specific type of influencers who show off their wealth, and those make the viewer feel jealousy for them.
