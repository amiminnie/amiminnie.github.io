How my Commissions work

13 apr 2026

For quite a long time, I've been that art friend in the friend group, being the go-to for whenever they needed something for their socials, or some project they've been working on. I always did these things free of charge for friends, but I've noticed their friends also starting to reach out to me. Because of that, I decided to open for commissions.

I really appreciate you checking my site out, and visiting this article!

What I do

Trying to learn different things all the time because well, I'm unemployed, I have accumulated a lot of skills over the years. Here I have listed some of the things I like to do, even if what you need is not listed, you can still reach me out on Discord to discuss more about your specific need.

Glow up your socials

One of the main things you need when making following on social media is good branding, and a banner plays a huge role in that. I can draw you a personalised banner, illustrated to capture your vibe. I have attached an example of my work, a banner made for my good friend S4qC.

Stylised 3D models

One of my dreams is to make a 3D game, every time I start a new project, I realise that I am not that great at the actual "making the game" part. But in the process, I have found one thing I'm actually good at, 3D modelling. What I have attached here is a screenshot from a game I'm working on, everything modelled by me. Not chasing complete realism and keeping my models stylised, I can give your game some character, a style that sets the mood!

Improve user experience

UI/UX design is yet another thing I learned from over the years, and I'm happy to help with your projects (Totally not because I don't have to do the coding part then XD). I have attached a picture of one of the user interfaces I designed for my long dead embedded system project, CrapWatch.

If you're wondering why I chose such a niche project to use as an example, I just haven't finished any of my games to the point where I had to design the UI, unfortunately...

Pricing

We can discuss more about this over on Discord. You can expect around 10 dollars for a normal socials banner or a set of 3D assets (kitchen tools for example).
