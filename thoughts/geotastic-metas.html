My Geotastic Meta Collection

01 may 2026 -last updated

Geotastic is undoubtedly my favourite game ever. There's just something about being dropped somewhere random almost anywhere in the world, and having to guess where you are. While it's true you can visit the same places just on Google Maps, the uncertainty of Geotastic makes it so much more fun. One round you could be dropped in the middle of London and the next round will be somewhere so random, like Northern Mariana Island.

To the outside viewer, it may look like I'm just super smart. In reality, I'm just remembering little metas that narrow down the location, or straight up just gives it away. I'm writing this article to list down the metas that help me find each country, in alphabetical order. I will be constantly updating this as I learn new things.

Albania

Albania is a pretty easy country to recognise, from the terrible job Google has done with the stitching of their panoramas. You'll find these "rifts" in the sky in pretty much everywhere in Albania. While it's also found in neighbouring North Macedonia, Albanian front plates have a blue strip on both sides, While North Macedonia uses the normal EU style.

Andorra

Andorra has these unique license plates with a yellow dot in the left. Other than that, it's mostly just vibes
